In order to report properly, we conform to the OSCRE Industry Data Model. OSCRE is the international standard for corporate real estate and real estate investment. To make sure your values end up in the right places, we need to map your columns to the database standard. Type the header of your upload in the appropriate boxes. You can overwrite the suggested column names.

Important notes:
  • Your upload must be in Excel format and have the .xlsx or .csv extension
  • In case of .xlsx, your workbook can only have 1 sheet
  • Your upload must have 1 header row
  • The format of your upload must be consistent; If the format of your upload changes, you will need to adjust the mapping here.
  • Feel free to reach out and we will send you an Excel template with explanations.
